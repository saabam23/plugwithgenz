What is Yoga?

Image by katemangostart on Freepik

Yoga was originated from Northern India. It is the practice of focusing on improving your flexibility, breathing, and strength. People also do yoga for their well-being and for exercising purposes. Here are some videos you could watch!

Why is breathing important for mental health?

Practicing taking deep breaths is a great strategy to help reduce stress and anxiety. When we take a breath, our brain is signaled to relax and reduce the tension we feel in our bodies. In order for this to work, it is important to breath at a pace that works for you! Here are some videos for guidance.
