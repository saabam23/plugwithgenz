WHAT IS MENTAL HEALTH?

According to the CDC (Centers for Disease Control and Prevention), mental health includes our social, psychological, and emotional well-being. It affects the way we think, feel, and act.

Mission Statement

PLUG is a reliable website and student-led organization that has a mission of spreading awareness on mental health to reduce the stigma and teaching teens life skills to help improve their well-being.
