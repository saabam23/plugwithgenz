WHY IS RELIEVING STRESS IMPORTANT?

sometimes when we are stressed, it is good to stop what we are doing, and find ways to focus on our thought process and what our body is telling us. in order to feel okay, it is important to seek your inner peace. maybe you are in a conflict and don't know how to approach it. or just simply need a space to vent. however, your response to stress matters most. this page is designated for healthy strategies to relieve your stress. it is good to not think too much and just breath.
