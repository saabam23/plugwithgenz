ABOUT PLUG

Plug was founded in 2021 as an idea to spread awareness on social injustice and reduce the mental health stigma. However, after reflecting on my virtual schooling experience, I was determined to find a way to create a support system for teens all over the country who struggled in the COVID-19 pandemic.

During the pandemic, it was so easy to turn to my phone and social media since I was closed off from nature and the world. I realized that looking at a screen for a long period of time made me forget the little things that mattered the most: life and a sense of shelter.

As a result, after learning the fundamentals of website development in the Girls Who Code Self Paced Program, I finally thought of the idea of coding a website that spreads awareness on mental health and provides resources that teaches teens life skills to improve their well-being. Additionally, I want to hold virtual and in-person events where people can participate in interactive activities to give them a start to discover their outlets and pave the way for them to seek professional help, if needed.

── Sydney Abam, Founder & Web Developer
